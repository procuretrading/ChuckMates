About SKOG TOOLS

Reliability Driven Excellence

Our Story

SKOG was founded on the principles of aviation utility and reliability. As a former commercial pilot and maintenance technician, founder Todd Evan operated in challenging environments where equipment diagnostics and alignment were essential.

Failure was not an option.

Systems and tools had to perform as expected, every time. This unwavering commitment to reliability became the foundation of SKOG TOOLS.

Todd's many years flying quality product is engineered with a focus on durability, quality, and performance. We prioritize manufacturing based on capability. The result is tools built to withstand demanding work and deliver confidence when it matters most.

Our Philosophy

When every second matters, reliability isn't just a feature—it's a necessity. Every SKOG TOOLS product is designed and tested to meet the highest standards of performance, ensuring that whether you're maintaining a commercial fleet or inflating your personal vehicle's tires, you have the confidence that comes from aviation-grade reliability.

Innovation That Matters

RatchetSeal™ Technology

Our patented RatchetSeal™ system in the Platinum Series provides an instant, leak-free seal that's easier than manually screwing on a chuck, yet more secure than traditional quick-connects.

SimpleSeal™ Technology

The Premium Series features SimpleSeal™ technology, eliminating the need for Teflon tape while maintaining superior reliability. Installation is truly easy-peasy.

Quality You Can Trust

Every SKOG TOOLS product undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our aviation-inspired standards. We use premium materials—from aerospace-grade aluminum in our Platinum Series to foam-resistant brass in our Premium line—because we know that quality materials lead to reliable performance.

When you choose SKOG TOOLS, you're choosing products that are engineered with the same attention to detail and commitment to excellence that keeps aircraft flying safely. That's the SKOG difference.
